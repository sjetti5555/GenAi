Dashboard Overview

Welcome to the Sentiment Analysis App, your one-stop platform for extracting insights from videos and comments. Our application offers powerful tools for video and keyword-based analysis, comment sentiment detection, and trend monitoring.

  • Video Analysis: Analyze the sentiment and key insights of a YouTube video by simply providing its URL. This feature processes video metadata, captions, and other content to give you a comprehensive overview.
  • Keyword Analysis: Search for videos using specific keywords and refine your search with filters like time frames (e.g., last 1 hour, 1 day). Perfect for targeted insights on trending topics.
  • Comments Analysis: Dive into the sentiment of a video's comments section by entering the YouTube URL. Understand audience sentiment, detect patterns, and gain actionable insights.
  • Quick Actions: Use the navigation tabs to access trending videos, saved reports, and support quickly and effortlessly.
